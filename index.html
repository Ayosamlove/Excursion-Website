Explore New Destinations

Florence, Italy

Noted for its culture, Renaissance art and architecture and monuments. The city contains numerous museums & art galleries, such as the Uffizi Gallery & Palazzo Pitti, and still exerts an influence in the fields of art, culture & politics.

Explore now!

Venice, Italy

Known as the 'City of Canals' many things Venice is famous for including its beautiful bridges, gondola rides, atmospheric streets and carnival celebrations. Built over 118 islands, Venice is one of the most unique cities in the world.

Explore now!

Milan, Italy

Milan is known for the majestic Duomo Cathedral, the outstanding opera La Scala, rich culture and the dazzling Vittorio Emanuele II shopping arcade. Its historical, cultural, and architectural landmarks makes it world-famous.

Explore now!

Rome, Italy

Say a more iconic capital city than Rome. I'll wait. Of course, you have to see the famous places in Rome, such as the Trevi fountain, the Vatican, the Pantheon or the great Colosseum! Rome is one of the most beautiful cities in the world.

Explore now!

Naples, Italy

Even if you've been to many Italian cities, nothing prepares you for the exuberant, colorful, and sometimes chaotic hubbub of Naples. It is a chaotic blend of centuries old history, magnificent architectures and charming streets.

Explore now!

Bologna, Italy

A gorgeous medieval centre, piles of perfect pasta and a world-renowned university, which is Europe's oldest, founded back as 1088. Bologna ticks all the boxes for a memorable stay combining great food & wine with good culture.
